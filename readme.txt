== Description ==

This is an extention to WooCommerce that allows you to place precious metals items for sale on your Wordpress/WooCommerce ecommerce store using the real time price of gold. Easily add markup, weight and purity of your items and you are good to go. You can be up and running in 10 minutes or less with real time pricing. Brought to you by www.gold-feed.com.

== Installation ==

*Using the WordPress dashboard*

1. Log into your WordPress as an administrator.
1. Click on "Plugins" then do a search for "WooCommerce Gold Price Live".
1. Click on "Install Now".
1. Activate the plugin.
1. Fill out the website information and contact information form and hit submit.
1. Add/Edit and existing WooCommerce product.
1. Fill out the general information for your product. Description and images etc.
1. Enter the purity and markup of your item in the "General" tab when adding or editing a product.
1. Enter the weight of your item in the "Shipping" tab in grams.
1. Update or publish your item or product.

*Manual*

1. Download the plugin from this page.
1. Login to your weblog administration area.
1. Click on "Plugins" then "Add New" and then "Upload".
1. Select the .zip file you just downloaded.
1. Activate the plugin.
1. Fill out the website information and contact information form and hit submit.
1. Add/Edit and existing WooCommerce product.
1. Fill out the general information for your product. Description and images etc.
1. Enter the purity and markup of your item in the "General" tab when adding or editing a product.
1. Enter the weight of your item in the "Shipping" tab in grams.
1. Update or publish your item or product.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How do I add or edit a product? =

After installing WooCommerce and WooCommerce Gold Price Live plugin when logged into your Wordpress dashboard as admin click on "Products" and then "Add New".

= Where do I add the markup of my item or product =

When adding or editing a product click on the "General" tab and there you will see a field for the "Purity" of the item.

= Where do I add the markup of my item or product =

When adding or editing a product click on the "General" tab and there you will see a field for the "Markup" of the item.

= Where do I add the weight of my item or product =

When adding or editing a product click on the "Shipping" tab and there you will see a field for the "Weight" of the item in grams.
